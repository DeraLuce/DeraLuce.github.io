Dera Luce

Logophile in every sense of the word / palabra / 言葉

Hi there. I'm Dera, a St. Louis-based writer and student by way of Los Angeles. I once captivated an entire room of people with a single utterance. An infant's first word is pretty exciting, I guess. I've expanded my vocabulary since then (in several languages), and now I love using words to tell stories. You can read more about me here, or follow me on Twitter here.
